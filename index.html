Our Mission

We are a collective of young visionaries committed to fostering critical thinking, promoting civic engagement, and developing leadership skills to build a stronger, more enlightened nation.

Who We Are

The Young Libyan Debating Society is a premier platform for young intellectuals dedicated to national development. Through rigorous debate and intellectual exchange, we aim to:

  • Develop solutions to national challenges
  • Cultivate ethical leadership qualities
  • Promote democratic values and civic responsibility
  • Encourage innovative thinking for social progress

National Discourse Series

Engage in structured debates on critical national issues, policy matters, and social development topics.

Leadership Labs

Interactive workshops focusing on public speaking, critical analysis, and ethical decision-making.

Youth Parliament

Simulated parliamentary sessions to understand governance and legislative processes.
